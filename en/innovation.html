Innovation

A Technological and Cognitive Approach

APES (Automated Profiling & Evaluation System) is a functional MVP designed to enhance cognitive scouting through behavioral profiling extracted from video footage and contextual social signals.

It does not replace human intuition—it amplifies it. APES helps scouts and performance staff identify promising players faster and more selectively, especially in youth categories and emerging markets.

Phase 2 will focus on improving its behavioral tagging engine and integrating real case studies from both video and social platforms.
